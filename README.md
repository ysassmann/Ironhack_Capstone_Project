# EvidenceAI: Evidence Chatbot for German Development Cooperation

 

## 1. Objective

EvidenceAI revolutionizes the handling of evidence in development cooperation. This AI-based chatbot systematically leverages knowledge from thousands of evaluation reports and project documents for the first time, thereby enhancing the impact and efficiency of development cooperation measures.

 

## 2. Why EvidenceAI Now?

 

### 2.1 The Untapped Potential of Our Evidence

Development cooperation possesses a unique treasure: over 10,000 evaluation reports and project documents containing valuable insights into success factors, proven approaches, and lessons learned. However, this treasure remains largely untapped:

- Evidence is not systematically utilized due to its sheer volume and fragmentation.

- Searching for knowledge is time-consuming and often unsuccessful.

- Institutional learning occurs only sporadically

- Planning and management processes are conducted without systematically referencing existing evidence.

 

The result: We "reinvent the wheel" repeatedly instead of building on proven solutions.

 

### 2.2 AI Opens New Possibilities

Generative AI makes the previously unthinkable possible: searching large volumes of text in seconds, extracting context-specific insights, and delivering tailored answers to complex questions. EvidenceAI is more than just a tool—it represents a paradigm shift towards an evidence-based, learning organization. The initial development phase lays the foundation for continuous advancement, keeping pace with the rapid evolution of AI.
